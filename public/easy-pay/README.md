# Easy Pay App

## Project Overview

Easy Pay is a peer-to-peer parking rental app designed to connect individuals who want to rent out their unused parking spaces with drivers looking for convenient parking solutions.

### Key Features
- **Real-time parking availability** - Find the nearest available parking spot quickly
- **Digital payment integration** - Secure online payment through digital banks and wallets
- **User-friendly interface** - Intuitive design for both parking space providers and seekers
- **Booking management** - View and manage parking bookings
- **Vehicle listing** - Add and manage your vehicle details for parking

## Problem Statement

In major urban areas, finding available parking is increasingly challenging, especially during peak hours. This leads to:
- Traffic congestion
- Wasted time searching for parking
- Increased fuel consumption
- Parking fines and theft risks for street parking
- Frustration among drivers

## Solution

Easy Pay Application provides a mobile-first solution that:
1. Allows property owners to monetize unused parking spaces
2. Helps drivers find convenient parking quickly
3. Facilitates secure digital transactions
4. Reduces overall parking demand through better space utilization
5. Promotes sustainable transportation options

## Design Process

### Research
- **Qualitative Research**: Conducted user surveys to understand pain points and preferences
- **User Personas**: Developed personas representing both parking providers and seekers
- **User Journey Mapping**: Mapped out complete user flows for both user types

### Design Phases
1. **Empathize** - User research and competitor analysis
2. **Define** - User personas and empathy mapping
3. **Ideate** - Brainstorming solutions and information architecture
4. **Design** - Wireframing and high-fidelity mockups
5. **Testing** - Usability testing and iterations

## App Flows

### For Parking Seekers
1. Search for parking spaces by address or map
2. View available parking spot details and pricing
3. Select parking time duration
4. Complete booking
5. Add vehicle information
6. Make payment

### For Parking Providers
1. List parking space details (location, price, availability)
2. Manage booking schedule
3. View booking requests
4. Confirm or decline bookings
5. Manage customer communications
